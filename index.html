TONE token: The fuel of the ecosystem

TONE.AI will launch an ERC-20 utility token to create a decentralized economy around AI-driven media revolution, allowing users to generate revenues from their face, voice, body, and more. TONE token will allow AI Miners to process raw data belonging to users (ranging from AI enthusiasts, endorsing celebrities and Web 3 believers) to create models. In return, the AI Miners will be compensated with TONE tokens, based on AI model fidelity and demand via our Genomic Consensus.
